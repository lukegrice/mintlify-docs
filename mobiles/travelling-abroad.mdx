### Travelling to Europe? 

<Check>
  Your data should work the same as it does at home as long as you have roaming switched on for your business sim. 
</Check>

![Screenshot2025 10 13142140 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-10-13142140.png)

<Warning>
  If you need minutes and texts to work, please submit a ticket.
</Warning>
